  • Phuket Gay Pride & Festival

    April 24 - May 1, 2016

    Colourful, eccentric, outrageous and full of fun, Phuket’s annual Gay Festival held in Patong is a riot of well-toned bodies on floats and in parades.

    November 14, 2016

    Loy Krathong is Thailand’s very own ‘festival of lights’. A national event, it is held annually on the evening of the 12th lunar month, usually in the first half of November.

    Vibrant celebrations and super shopping bargains abound as Phuket's local Chinese community prepares for its most important festival.
